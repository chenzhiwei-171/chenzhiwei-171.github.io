My research explores electromagnetic counterparts associated with compact-binary mergers, especially binary neutron-star mergers.

The main topics include:

- kilonova and merger-nova emission;
- short gamma-ray bursts and extended emission;
- fallback accretion and fallback-powered transients;
- relativistic jet propagation and breakout;
- the influence of the merger remnant on electromagnetic emission;
- joint gravitational-wave and electromagnetic detectability.

The aim is to connect the properties of merger remnants, ejecta, and relativistic outflows with observable electromagnetic transients.
