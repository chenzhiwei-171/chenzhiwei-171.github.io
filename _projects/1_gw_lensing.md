My research investigates the gravitational lensing of gravitational waves from compact-binary mergers. This includes strong lensing by galaxies and galaxy clusters, microlensing by compact objects, and the identification of host galaxies of lensed gravitational-wave sources.

The main topics include:

- detection rates of strongly lensed gravitational-wave events;
- localization and host-galaxy identification;
- lensed multi-messenger signals;
- cosmological inference using time delays and lensing information;
- microlensing and wave-optics effects in gravitational-wave signals.

This work combines gravitational-wave population modelling, lensing statistics, galaxy catalogues, and multi-messenger follow-up strategies.
