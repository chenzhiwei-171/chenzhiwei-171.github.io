My research investigates the gravitational lensing of gravitational waves from compact-binary mergers.

The main topics include:

- strongly lensed gravitational-wave events;
- host-galaxy identification;
- lensed multi-messenger signals;
- cosmological inference;
- microlensing and wave-optics effects.
